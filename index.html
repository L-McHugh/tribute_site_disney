Who was the founder?

The main visionary behind Disney was a man called Walt Disney. He was born in 1901 and died in 1966. He was the founder of Disneyland and Walt Disney World resorts. In addition, he created animation classics such as Mickey Mouse.

The parks

There are 12 Disney parks located all over the world to make it easier for people to experience the Disney magic. These are:

  • California Adventure
  • Disney’s Animal Kingdom
  • Disney’s Hollywood Studios
  • Disneyland
  • Disneyland Paris
  • EPCOT
  • Hong Kong Disneyland
  • Magic Kingdom
  • Shanghai Disneyland
  • Tokyo Disney Sea
  • Tokyo Disneyland
  • Walt Disney Studios Park

Animation

Disney has created many classic films and animations. The first film it made was Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs in 1937. The highest grossing Walt Disney animation movie was Frozen 2 in 2019 making £1.22 billion.
